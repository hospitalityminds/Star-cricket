About Star Cricket Academy

Star Cricket Academy has an aim and vision to nurture talent and transform budding cricketers into professionals. The academy focuses on cricket and fitness of the individuals. Star Cricket Academy trains kids from the age of 5 years and above up to 20 years in both girls and boy’s category.
